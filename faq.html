What is this?

Wedding is your special moment and we really understand that. We provide service to create your wedding website with elegant, fresh, romantic theme. Choose a theme and the website is ready for you right away.

How does it work?

Choose a theme from our THEMES page and contact our marketing/sales team. We will contact you about the details and your website will soon be ready in short waiting time. However, we need you to help us with several things like

  • Website content / text. For your special moment we need you to compose several wordings for your website
  • Photos
  • Wedding information (venue, program, etc)
  • Accommodations list, travel info, etc.
  • Do you need RSVP?
  • Other information based on your theme chosen or your special request.

What will I get?

Full working website with

  • Chosen theme and layout
  • Content / text following the pattern of chosen theme.
  • Photo Gallery
  • Integrated Maps
  • RSVP system
  • Custom Domain (if requested, with additional domain fee)
  • Others based on chosen theme

How long does it take to create my website?

Depends on the complexity, but it is usually between 3-5 days.

What's included in the theme price?

  • A working website, full hosted and ready to serve information to your beloved friends and families

  • Image entry and manipulation (if necessary) service
